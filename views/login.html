How does the "magic" happen?

For best integration with Evernote, a new notebook named "BlogWith" will appear in your notebook list.
Whenever you create or edit a new note inside this special "BlogWith" notebook, BlogWith will sync your note to your blog.

Simple and pleasant way to add images into a post

Just drag and drop your images into Evernote. No more uploading then inserting links.
(To make the image displayed faster for readers, you may consider to resize the image before adding it into Evernote)

Edit a post is easier than ever.

Just edit your local note and sync. No more opening, loading and previewing.
This works perfectly for fixing your typo mistakes, and any quick editing while reviewing your post.
